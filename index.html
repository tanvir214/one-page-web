The concept

Lendino is a marketplace for loans where private and professional investors can lend directly to creditworthy companies.

Local loans

Lendino's loan market enables loans between companies and investors with local knowledge. Lendino thus strengthens local affiliation.

Better financing

Lendino allows companies to borrow directly from Danish investors. This provides higher returns for lenders and lower costs for borrowers
